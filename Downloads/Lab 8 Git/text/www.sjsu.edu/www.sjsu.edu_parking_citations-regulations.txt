Citations and Regulations
Responsibility for establishing rules and regulations regarding the operation and
                  parking of vehicles on university property is given to the University President pursuant
                  to Section 21113 (a) of the California Vehicle Code (CVC). 
A valid parking permit is required in all campus parking garages and parking lots.
                  You can buy permits at any Permit Pay-Station inside any of the 3 garages or at our
                  office.  Permit must be purchased as soon as you parked.
Parking regulations are enforced 24-hours a day, 7 days a week, every day, including
                  holidays. The minimum parking citation fine is $23.
